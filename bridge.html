Ord/Eth Bridge

Bridge Bitcoin Ordinals to Ethereum NFTs and back again. See Collection on OpenSea. Join our Discord.

Our bridge now supports BRC-20 tokens: you can filter by tokens and which ones are on sale on your favorite marketplace (e.g. OpenSea). Each token comes with a link in its description to verify the vault's balance. BRC-20 tokens cannot be spent on Bitcoin while they are bridged to Ethereum.
